About Monika

Curious about numbers before I was curious about marketing.

I got into digital marketing almost by accident — I liked figuring out why one social post did better than another, and that question never really left me. A year later, it's still what gets me up in the morning: a dashboard full of numbers waiting to be explained.

My story

From intern to independent.

Digital marketing wasn't something I planned out years in advance — it's something I grew into by doing it. During my internship at JClick Solutions, I got to sit in on real client accounts: writing ad copy that had to actually convert, watching keyword rankings move week by week, and learning that "engagement" is a number you can influence on purpose, not something that just happens.

That internship is where the fundamentals stuck — how to read a campaign report properly, how to talk about strategy without hiding behind jargon, and how much of good marketing is just paying close attention. When it ended, I decided to keep going on my own, and freelancing let me apply everything I'd learned directly, project by project, client by client.
